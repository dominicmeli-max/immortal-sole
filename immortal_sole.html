Cyclon™ Loop
Circularity

Shredded every time.
Reborn every time.
No mysticism.

Just chemistry and a choice. The Cyclon™ loop is a closed system — nothing enters landfill, nothing is wasted. Every gram of material you wear today will be part of tomorrow's shoe.

  1. Lab · Zürich — Castor bean extract polymerised into Helion™ foam. LightSpray™ upper formed by robot. Zero offcuts.
  2. Wear — Run your marathon. Live your life. The shoe is built for performance, not preservation.
  3. Return — Drop it in the return box or request collection. We handle everything. No sorting, no guilt.
  4. Shred + Reform — Industrial shredder. Granulation. Pelletisation. The same atoms, ready for their next life.
  5. Rebirth — Your new pair ships. Same performance. Different life. The loop continues.
